## ðŸ”„ Workflow

> **Source:** [Semrush Keyword Strategy](https://www.semrush.com/blog/keyword-research-guide/) & [Ahrefs SEO Guide](https://ahrefs.com/blog/keyword-research/)

### Phase 1: Topic Discovery (Seed)
- [ ] **Brainstorm**: Identify main topics (Topic Clusters) aligned with business goals.
- [ ] **Competitor**: Analyze keywords competitors are getting traffic from (Gap Analysis).
- [ ] **Questions**: Collect question patterns from "People Also Ask" and forums (Reddit/Quora).

### Phase 2: Metric Analysis
- [ ] **Volume vs Difficulty**: Find High volume + Low KD (Keyword Difficulty) opportunities (Low Hanging Fruit).
- [ ] **Intent Matching**: Determine the intent of the keyword (Navigational, Informational, Transactional).
- [ ] **Long-tail**: Focus on 3+ word phrases with high conversion rates.

### Phase 3: Mapping & Clustering
- [ ] **Pillar Page**: Plan a comprehensive guide covering the main keyword.
- [ ] **Cannibalization**: Ensure there aren't multiple pages targeting the same keyword.

### Checkpoints
| Phase | Verification                                                          |
| ----- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1     | Does our content match the search intent of the target keyword?       |
| 2     | Is our domain authority sufficient to rank for this keyword?          |
| 3     | Do we offer something "better" (10x Content) than competitor content? |
